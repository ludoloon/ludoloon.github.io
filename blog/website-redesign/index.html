LudoLoon Studio Redesign

Hello everyone!

I've recently decided to pursue my passion for music more seriously. If you don't know already, I've been putting up music for almost a decade now as Groove Masta Putz. Originally I was going to just put up video game music under the same name, but I decided that there are different audiences I'm approaching for these projects.

This side of my music projects will be focused on music for video game studios as a composer. I will leave Groove Masta Putz as a Solo project dedicated to the people who have been following my music there. It will be more personal and less tailored to business.

That's pretty much it! Keep an eye out.
