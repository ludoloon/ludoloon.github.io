Game jam project: Bastard Tower

2023/02/19

I helped make the music for the game jam project Bastard Tower!

It was created for the Brackeys Game Jam 2023.1 by The Great DS.

Features some upbeat boss music vibes as well as ambient builder music that I'm very pleased with :)

Summary

From the project's itch page:

The world has been absolutely dominated by stupid demonic creatures, now the last humans must fight them and survive their army of evil!

Order your team and build a high tower to protect your humans from the demons.

You are going to DIE in the end.. But hopefuly the next group of survivors may finish the tower.
