Game jam project: Bastard Tower

2024/04/16

I helped make the music for the game jam project Bastard Tower!

It was created for the Brackeys Game Jam 2023.1 by The Great DS.

Features some upbeat boss music vibes as well as ambient builder music that I'm very pleased with :)

Summary

PS2 Graveyards is an ode to the sounds that inspired me playing games like King's Field and Silent Hill 2 growing up, and the dying past they represent. Definitely a bit of a danceable take, but there's still some good old fashioned ambience too.

So go find a graveyard you love and put this shit on lol.
