About

Hi! My name is Pierce and I make music as LudoLoon Studio.

I've always loved listening to video game music since I was a kid. Stuff like sonic, mega man, sim city 3000, bomberman hero, diablo, and other classics we all love. Now I'm excited to finally be making it myself.

My favorite genres are:

  • Electronic music (everything from dance to downtempo)
  • Ambient atmospheric journeys
  • Classic sounding VGM
  • Hip-hop beats
  • Jazz, Funk and Blues inspirations
  • Whatever else sounds good!

You can browse my site to hear the sounds I have to offer for yourself.

If you want regular royalty free music for your projects then either subscribe to my patreon or the email list below.

My general process for making music works like this:

  • Negotiate the terms.
  • Get the references for the desired vibe from you.
  • Create my sound palette. Compose first drafts of the songs.
  • Add more layers after revision, until it's to your taste.
  • Mix & master the track. Send it back.
  • Rinse & repeat until the track is perfect!
