About

LudoLoon Studio LLC is a project I originally started with the intention of becoming a game development studio. However, in the process of trying to make a game all by myself, I learned that it's a lot of work!

I decided it's best to stick to my strengths and work primarily on music. I've been making music for many years under the name Groove Masta Putz, but since this is a much different project than my other work it made sense to separate the two.

I may still decide to work on video games as a dev, but for now if you have any music needs then please feel free to reach out. Let's work together!
